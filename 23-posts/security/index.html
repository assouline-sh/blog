CompTIA Security+ Certification

2023.01.20
certification
Home / '23 Posts / CompTIA Security+ Certification

Passed Security+! After consistently studying all of Fall semester, I hunkered down during the last week of winter break to cram as many practice tests as possible. It was important to me to set a goal and steadily move towards it to help build the habit of continuous self improvement and learning. The content supplemented my coursework nicely, and often acted as a reinforcement. I enjoyed that the questions pushed me to apply my conceptual knowledge to realistic scenarios.

Security+ is only the beginning, and I’m excited to continue onto more advanced certifications!

Here are some of the main concepts covered:

  • Cryptography
  • Malicious code
  • Secure coding
  • Security assessment and training
  • Social engineering, physical, and password attacks
  • Identity and access management
  • Cloud and virtualization security
  • Endpoint security
  • Network security
  • Wireless and mobile security
  • Incident response
  • Digital forensics
  • Enterprise risk management
